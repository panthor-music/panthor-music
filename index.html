The Artist

Born in the musical city of Kingston, Panthor began his musical journey in the seventies and migrated to the US in 1971. There, he attended Eastern District High School and went on to receive formal voice training at Kingsborough Community College, where he sang in the choir.

His first recording was done at the legendary Bullwackies Recording Studio. Over the years, he has performed at popular clubs in Brooklyn, pool parties in Queens and Long Island, and sang with both the Brimstone Band and the Melody Vibez Band.

Panthor has graced the stages of several premier hotels and venues, including the Bahia Principe, FDR, Treasure Beach, Travellers Inn, Tek It Easy, and Amnesia Night Club, to name a few. Currently working with some of the industry's greatest musicians, music has always been a passionate love for him. He gives continuous thanks to the Almighty for life, health, and strength.
