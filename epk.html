Artist Biography

Panthor — Official Press Photo

Born in the musical city of Kingston, Panthor began his musical journey in the seventies and migrated to the US in 1971. There, he attended Eastern District High School and received formal voice training at Kingsborough Community College, where he sang in the choir.

His first recording was done at the legendary Bullwackies Recording Studio in Brooklyn — a cornerstone of New York's reggae scene. Over the years, he has performed at popular clubs in Brooklyn, pool parties in Queens and Long Island, and sang with both the Brimstone Band and the Melody Vibez Band.

Panthor has graced the stages of several premier hotels and venues in Jamaica, including the Bahia Principe, FDR, Treasure Beach, Travellers Inn, Tek It Easy, and Amnesia Night Club. He brings a warm, soulful energy to every stage — from intimate lounges to large-scale events.

Currently working with some of the industry's greatest musicians, Panthor continues to create music rooted in authentic Caribbean culture. His debut catalog of eleven original, fully mastered tracks is available now. Music has always been a passionate love for him — he gives thanks to the Almighty for life, health, and strength.
